local-harness

Use the AI subscriptions and local models you already have, inside VS Code, Claude Code & the terminal — privately, on your own machine.

Your AI subscriptions

Have a Claude or Gemini subscription? Sign in below and this page shows you exactly how to use it in your coding tools — as an extra option that never touches the models already built into those tools.

Your local model

Running a model on your own machine (Ollama, LM Studio, vLLM, …)? Enter its address and this page shows how to use it in your tools. Everything stays on your computer — it never touches the internet.

More options (advanced — use a browser login)

For subscriptions with no official sign-in for tools (Gemini when your account can't use its CLI, and Perplexity). These use unofficial helpers that reuse your browser login — more powerful, but riskier: you're handing a full-account login to third-party software. Prefer a spare account if you can.
